Digital Marketing Basics

MODULE 02

Marketing Strategy & Funnel

Ad podradhukku munnadi, plan pannanum. Idhu illama ad podradhu — vazhi theriyama drive pannina maadhiri.

🛒 The Marketing Funnel (TOFU - MOFU - BOFU)

  • 1
    TOFU (Top of Funnel) — Awareness: People unga brand-ah therinju kollanum. Reels, awareness ads, brand videos.
  • 2
    MOFU (Middle) — Consideration: Unga product-ah research pannuranga. Testimonials, comparison posts, retargeting ads.
  • 3
    BOFU (Bottom) — Conversion: Buy panna ready. Offer ads, "limited stock", direct WhatsApp/call CTA.
Real Example — Madurai Fashion Store

TOFU la — "Trending kurta styles 2026" reel (1L views). MOFU la — andha reel paathavanga ku retargeting ad "ithu dhaan unga size la available". BOFU la — "Ipo order pannina free delivery, 2 days mattum" ad. Indha 3 step combo la conversion rate 3x aagum.

👤 Customer Avatar (Target Audience)

Ad podradhukku munnadi indha 4 questions answer pannunga:

  • Yaaru unga ideal customer? (age, gender, location)
  • Avanga problem enna? (pain point)
  • Avanga eppo, eppadi unga product-ah use pannuvanga?
  • Edhukaaga avanga COMPETITOR-kitta illa unga kitta vaanganum?
Example

"Coimbatore la irukra 25-40 vayasu women, working professionals, weight loss interest, premium healthy snacks vaanga ready" — idhu oru clear avatar.

🧭 Google Search Console (GSC) — Complete Setup

GSC na — Google-oda direct connection. Unga website Google la eppadi perform aaguthu, eth keyword la varudhu, eth pages la errors irukku — ellam idhula free ah paakalam. Idhu illama, unga site Google search la varadhu correct ah track panna mudiyadhu.

  • 1
    search.google.com/search-console la Google account use panni login pannunga
  • 2
    Property add pannunga — 2 options varum:
    • Domain property (recommended) — "ammatiffins.com" — http/https/www ellam cover aagum, but DNS verification venum
    • URL prefix property — "https://www.ammatiffins.com" — easy verification (HTML file / meta tag)
  • 3
    Ownership verify pannunga — easiest method:
    • HTML tag method: Google kodutha meta tag-ah copy panni, WordPress nu "Site Kit by Google" plugin la paste pannunga (no-code, 1 click)
    • DNS method: Domain provider (Hostinger/GoDaddy) DNS settings la TXT record add pannunga — domain property ku idhu use aagum
    • HTML file upload: Google kodutha file-ah website root folder la upload pannunga (cPanel File Manager)
  • 4
    Verify button click pannunga — "Ownership verified" message vandha, setup success

🗺️ Sitemap Create & Submit

Sitemap na — unga website la irukra ellam pages-oda oru "list/map", Google ku kaattradhu — "idhu ellam pages irukku, idha crawl pannunga" nu.

  • 1
    WordPress users: Yoast SEO or Rank Math plugin install pannunga (free) — sitemap automatic ah generate aagum at yourdomain.com/sitemap_index.xml (Yoast) or /sitemap.xml (Rank Math)
  • 2
    Wix/Shopify/Framer: Sitemap auto-generate aagum — Settings → SEO la link kaattum, no manual work venam
  • 3
    Andha sitemap URL-ah browser la open panni check pannunga — pages list show aaganumnu
  • 4
    Search Console la — left menu → Sitemaps → "Add a new sitemap" → sitemap URL paste pannunga (e.g., sitemap_index.xml) → Submit
  • 5
    Status "Success" nu varum — couple of days la Google pages-ah crawl panna start pannum

📋 GSC — Important Reports to Check Weekly

  • Performance report: Eeth keyword la unga site varudhu, clicks, impressions, average position
  • Coverage / Pages report: Eth pages indexed aachu, eth pages "Excluded" or errors irukku
  • URL Inspection tool: Specific page-ah paste panni "Request Indexing" pannalam — new page fast ah Google la varum
  • Core Web Vitals: Site speed/experience score — mobile usability errors kaatum
  • Links report: Eppadi backlinks/internal links irukku nu paakalam

Meta Ads — Strategy, Targeting & Optimization

👥 Audience Types — Detailed

  • Core Audience: Location + Age + Gender + Interest (cold audience)
  • Custom Audience: Unga existing customers (phone list, website visitors, video viewers)
  • Lookalike Audience: Unga best customers maadhiri irukravanga-ah Meta automatic ah find pannum
  • Advantage+ Audience: AI automatic targeting (Meta recommend pannudhu, beginners-ku easy)
Real Example — Skincare Brand

Phase 1: Cold audience (Interest: skincare, beauty) → 500 purchases data collect. Phase 2: Lookalike 1% (similar to buyers) → CPA 40% kammi aanadhu.

🎨 Ad Creative — Hook Formula

First 3 seconds la stop pannanum (scroll-stopping). Indha formula use pannunga:

  • 1
    Hook (0-3 sec): Problem or shocking statement — "Unga AC bill romba high ah irukka?"
  • 2
    Solution (3-15 sec): Product demo, before/after
  • 3
    Proof (15-25 sec): Reviews, results, "5000+ customers"
  • 4
    CTA (25-30 sec): "Link la click pannunga" / "WhatsApp ku message pannunga"

📊 Key Metrics to Track (Tanglish)

MetricEnna nu paakanumGood Range (general)

CPL (Cost per Lead)Oru lead ku evlo costBusiness margin paathu compare pannunga

International Selling — Export & Tax Deep-Dive

Etsy/Amazon Global Selling vazhi velinaadu customers ku sell panna — basic GST/IEC mention pannirundhom (Module 29). Idhu andha topic-ah deeper ah explain pannudhu — LUT, GST on exports, payment receiving methods.

📋 Key Documents/Registrations for Export

  • IEC (Import Export Code): Free, apply via DGFT website (dgft.gov.in) — mandatory for export business beyond personal/gift shipments. PAN-based, takes 1-2 days online
  • GST Registration: Required — exports are treated as "zero-rated supply" under GST, meaning no GST charged to foreign buyer, but you must be registered to claim this
  • LUT (Letter of Undertaking): File once a year on GST portal — allows you to export without paying IGST upfront (otherwise you'd pay IGST and claim refund later, which is slower)
  • AD Code Registration: Your bank's Authorized Dealer code, registered with customs — needed for receiving export payments and shipping via courier (DHL/FedEx/India Post)

💳 Receiving International Payments

  • Payoneer: Most common for Etsy sellers in India — Etsy pays into Payoneer, withdraw to Indian bank account (small conversion fee)
  • Amazon Global Selling: Amazon handles payment collection in buyer's currency, converts and deposits to your linked Indian bank via their payment service
  • PayPal: Some marketplaces support it — but Etsy in India has restrictions on PayPal for receiving (varies, check current Etsy policy)
  • FIRC (Foreign Inward Remittance Certificate): Your bank/Payoneer provides this — keep for tax records, proves the income is export revenue

🔄 Workflow: Setting Up for Export (First-Time)

  • 1
    Apply for IEC on dgft.gov.in (free, online, PAN + Aadhaar based)
  • 2
    Ensure GST registration is active (Module 29)
  • 3
    File LUT on GST portal (Services → User Services → Furnish Letter of Undertaking) — valid for one financial year, renew annually
  • 4
    Register AD Code with your bank — bank submits this to customs for your business
  • 5
    Set up Payoneer (for Etsy) — link to Indian bank account, complete KYC
  • 6
    Mark invoices as "Export under LUT, without payment of IGST" — your CA/accountant will know this phrase for invoicing software
  • 7
    File GST returns — export sales reported in GSTR-1 under "zero-rated supplies"
Real Example — Handicraft Exporter (Etsy)

Seller selling wooden home decor on Etsy to US/UK buyers. Initially didn't have IEC/LUT — was paying IGST on every "export" and claiming refunds (2-3 month delay, cash flow problem). After filing LUT — no IGST paid upfront, cash flow improved immediately. CA helped file LUT in one sitting (free on GST portal, just needs digital signature or EVC via OTP).

⚠️ Important Notes

✓
This is general guidance, not legal/tax advice — always consult a CA for your specific business structure (proprietorship vs company affects some details)
✓
Income tax: Export income is still taxable under Income Tax Act (GST zero-rating ≠ income tax exemption) — report all marketplace income
✓
Shipping/customs: For physical goods, check HS codes for your product category — affects customs declarations

Website Accessibility (WCAG Basics) — Compliance & SEO

Accessibility na — unga website-ah, disabilities (vision, hearing, motor) irukravanga kooda use panna mudiyum nu ensure panradhu. Idhu "nice to have" mattum illa — legal requirement (many countries), AND Google ranking factor kooda.

⚖️ Why Accessibility Matters

  • Legal compliance: US (ADA), EU (EAA), India (RPWD Act guidelines) — businesses can face lawsuits/complaints for inaccessible websites, especially e-commerce
  • Larger audience: 15%+ of global population has some disability — accessible site = more potential customers
  • SEO overlap: Alt text, heading structure, descriptive links — same things Google uses for SEO (Module 04) ARE accessibility basics
  • Better UX for everyone: Clear contrast, readable fonts, keyboard navigation — helps all users, not just those with disabilities

📐 WCAG — The 4 Core Principles (POUR)

  • Perceivable: Content users-ku "perceive" panna mudiyanum — images ku alt text, videos ku captions
  • Operable: Keyboard mattum use panni navigate panna mudiyanum (mouse illama)
  • Understandable: Clear language, predictable navigation, form errors clearly explained
  • Robust: Different devices/assistive tech (screen readers) la work aaganum

WCAG levels: A (minimum), AA (standard — most businesses target this), AAA (highest, often impractical for full sites)

🛠️ Step-by-Step: Make Your Site WCAG AA-Friendly (No-Code)

  • 1
    Run a free audit: WAVE (wave.webaim.org) — paste your URL, get a visual report of issues (errors in red, warnings in yellow)
  • 2
    Add alt text to all images: Describe what's in the image, not just "image1.jpg" — e.g., "Red cotton kurta with floral print, front view" (also helps SEO — Module 04)
  • 3
    Fix color contrast: Text vs background contrast ratio should be 4.5:1 minimum for normal text. Use WebAIM Contrast Checker (free tool) — light gray text on white background usually fails
  • 4
    Heading structure: One H1 per page, then H2, H3 in logical order — don't skip levels (helps screen readers AND SEO)
  • 5
    Descriptive link text: Avoid "Click here" — use "Download the pricing guide" so screen reader users understand the link's purpose out of context
  • 6
    Form labels: Every input field should have a visible label (not just placeholder text, which disappears when typing)
  • 7
    Video captions: Add captions to video content — YouTube auto-generates these, review and correct them
  • 8
    Keyboard navigation test: Try navigating your site using only Tab/Enter keys (no mouse) — can you reach the menu, forms, buttons?
  • 9
    WordPress plugin: "WP Accessibility" or "One Click Accessibility" — adds skip links, contrast tools, focus outlines automatically
Real Example — E-commerce Store

Fixed: added alt text in bulk (helped SEO too — 3 product pages started ranking for new keywords within a month), changed button to darker blue (passed contrast, also improved visibility → conversion rate up slightly), rewrote links to "View [Product Name] details".
